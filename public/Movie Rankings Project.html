Amature Movie Critic Dylan Bryants Top 3 Movies

Top 3 Movies 2007-2023

Hot Rod

Copyright © 2007 Paramount Pictures. All rights reserved.

A truly delightful comedy that revolves around a stuntman raising money to save his step fathers life. I have a soft spot for it in my heart. It was a movie that everyone in my family got to enjoy together.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Copyright © 2021 Marvel Studios. All rights reserved.

Such an enjoyable experience. Even more so if you're a fan of the previous Spider-Man movies. It really ties everything together from previous films in a way that will constantly have you smiling.

Dune

Copyright © 2021 Legendary Pictures. All rights reserved.
Dune is a top tier sci-fi film based off a beloved book seires. The visuals alone could sell anyone on the movie. The only negative being it's the first of two movies so as a stand-alone movie it is incomplete.
